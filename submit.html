Submission

Use the steps below to submit your game prototype to the CS Night Game Jam competition.

Instructions

Follow these steps to submit your app.

  1. Download the source for your App Inventor project. Go to "My Projects" and click "More Actions" » "Download Source" and download the source to your computer.

  2. Email gamejam@cs.usfca.edu with your downloaded source. Please use "Game Jam Submission" as the subject, and CC all of your team members.

  3. Wait for the confirmation email. We will manually respond via email once we have received your submission. If you do not get a response within 15 minutes, please try again.

We will judge the submissions during CS Night, and announce the winners after the State of the Department address. Team members must be present to receive their prizes!

Upload App (Optional)

Show off your game in the App Inventor Community Gallery! The steps are:

  1. Take a screenshot of your app running in the emulator. If you are on a lab computer, see 4 Methods to Take Screenshot Capture in Ubuntu Linux for how to take a screenshot of the emulator window.

  2. Download the source for your App Inventor project. Go to "My Projects" and click "More Actions" » "Download Source" and download the source to your computer.

  3. Sign in to the App Inventor Community Gallery. You can use the same account you used for App Inventor.

  4. Upload your app to the gallery. Go to "My Apps" and click "Add New Application" to upload your app. Fill in the necessary information and upload your source file and screenshot. Click "Save Application" when done.

This part is optional, but this will allow other people to be able to download and play your app! If you choose this route, please include a link to your app in your submission email.
